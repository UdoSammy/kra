KRA FAQs

By creating a conducive environment for the flourishing of the African child, Kids Reach Africa Foundation hopes to provide African children with access to quality education and ensure all the requirements for effective formal education are available and utilized, regardless of their colour, sex, height, or other circumstances of their birth.

Children resident in rural African societies and communities are eligible beneficiaries of the activities of the Kids Reach Africa Foundation. There are no special conditions to be a beneficiary other than to be recommended by our unbiased outreach team and to not be over the age of 18.

There are no restrictions or limitations for the support of Kids Reach Africa Foundation other than age. Any child who is not more than the age of 18 at the time of being recommended for Kids Reach Africa Foundation shall be beneficiary of the support for as long a time as the support may last or till the child gets to 19 years of age.

Kids Reach Africa Foundation is currently operable in West African Countries. Given her goals and objectives, Kids Reach Africa Foundation soon hopes to extend to all other African regions and countries.

Kids Reach Africa Foundation is first funded by its members and directors. Third party supports and sponsors – consisting of individuals, local and international organized bodies, and government bodies – make up the other major sponsors.

Kids Reach Africa Foundation is managed and run by its executive team, consisting of its board of trustees and outreach team with no gender or race preferences.

Partnership or Sponsorship is open to anybody, group of people or body that is committed to help Kids Reach Africa Foundation achieve her set goals.

Partnerships with Kids Reach Africa Foundation is possible at different levels, ranging from Monthly, 3 Monthsly, Half-Yearly Yearly, or Community Based. Use this link to PARTNER WITH US or SPONSOR US.
